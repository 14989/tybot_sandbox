Roadmap

  • Gathered Requirements

    1 week

    We met with key stakeholders in Marketing oporation to determine TyBot requirements.

  • Created Development Plan

    1 week

    We created a development plan, and documented the project using Confluence.

  • Built TyBot

    4 weeks

    We decided on three main components:

    • TyBots Framework on AWS
    • Normalize Email Address Field Values
    • Match Leads to Accounts Based on Email Domain

  • TyBot is Live in Sandbox

    July 16th, 2020

    TyBot was deployed to AWS. TyBot runs and updates records nightly.

  • TyBot Grows When Fed

    Q4 2020

    With continued resource, TyBot can grow into a powerful automation platform for Tyler Marketing. New flows take roughly 10 hours of development time. Below are examples of what we can acomplish in Q4 2020:

    • Deploy to Production
    • Update Country Based on Email Domain

  • Connect TyBot to More Systems

    Q1 2021

    TyBot can connect to any system with an API. Below are some examples:

    • Create Programs/Campaigns directly from Eventuazity/iCapture
    • List Management: create leads and add to list directly from Power BI or CRM
    • More Data Cleaning: Use email doamin to update all account info
    • Asset Management
